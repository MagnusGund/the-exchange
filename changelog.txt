Version: 0.1.0
Date: 2026-01-29
  Features:
    - Initial release of Incremental Industrialist
    - Trade Hub entity for managing the incremental trade system
    - Import Chests that generate resources and output to adjacent belts
    - Export Chests that accept products to fulfill orders
    - Order system with tiered complexity (small to massive orders)
    - Progressive order unlocks based on research completion
    - Currency system with natural inflation scaling
    - Upgrade shop with categories:
      - Import Upgrades (rate, material unlocks, tier upgrades)
      - Export Upgrades (order slots)
      - Factory Bonuses (assembler speed, inserter speed, mining productivity, lab speed)
      - Robot Bonuses (cargo size, speed) - requires robotics research
    - Full GUI with Orders, Upgrades, Imports, and Statistics tabs
    - Localization support (English)
    - Technology unlocks: Trade Operations (green science), Advanced Imports, Robot Logistics
